# Adding notes to pages

At design time, you may need to keep some notes (just for you, they won't be published).

1. Open the Notes panel on the right side or press Alt+N
1. Type a note in the text area at the bottom of the panel
1. Press Enter
1. The note will be attached to your current page.

You can change the color of each note by clicking with the right button.

Select the color you want in the context menu.

You also can copy the text to the clipboard or delete the note.
