# Cookie consent

The Cookie consent is mandatory on almost all websites because of the GDPR EU law. It warns your visitors that your
website uses Cookies to store information about them.

Even if you didn't code anything special about user tracking on your website, many services such as Google Analytics,
Social networks and Video services store cookies to track your visitors. That's why you should absolutely activate this
feature.

> Note: If you disable the Cookie consent feature, some services may not work properly, such as Google Analytics,
> Disqus...

| Option                                | Description                                                                                     |
|---------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Show the cookie consent flyout        | Activate this option to display the flyout on your pages                                        |
| Force the consent to view the content | Activate this option if the user must accept or adjust the cookie settings to view your content |

## Consent flyout settings

The consent flyout is the first thing your visitors will see when they open your website for the first time, it will
tell your visitors that your website uses the cookie technology to store information about their visit on their device.

You can customize the type of component to use for this feature, as well as the position of the flyout.

## Settings panel settings

The Cookie Settings panel is where your visitors can enable or disable some services that store cookies on their
computer.

By default, PageFabric allows your website to store Marketing cookies (for Google Analytics) and Social cookies (for the
Disqus Part).

You can customize the type of component to use for this feature, as well as the position of the panel.

## Linguistic resources

By default, all text resources are in English. You should type your own texts in each supported language of your
website.
